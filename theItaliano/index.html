About Us

At Italiano, we believe that great food starts with the finest ingredients, combined with passion, tradition, and a love for Italian cuisine. Our family-owned restaurant has been serving authentic Italian dishes for over 5 years, bringing the rich flavors of Italy to the heart of Mumbai.

We invite you to experience the true taste of Italy with us, where every meal is a celebration of tradition, warmth, and unforgettable flavors.
